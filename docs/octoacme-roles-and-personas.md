# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
QA Leads ensure quality standards are met, oversee the testing process, and coordinate defect remediation with the development team.

### Responsibilities
- Design and execute comprehensive test plans
- Oversee test automation and quality metrics
- Coordinate defect triage and prioritization
- Validate acceptance criteria before release
- Lead quality retrospectives and process improvements

### Goals
- Maintain high product quality and reliability
- Reduce defect escape rate to production
- Enable fast, confident deployments

### Typical Communication
- Sprint reviews and defect triage sessions with Developers and Project Managers
- Test plan and quality metrics reports
- Pre-release sign-off and validation checklists

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers design user interfaces, validate usability, and ensure products are intuitive and accessible. They collaborate with Product Managers to understand user needs and with Developers to ensure design feasibility.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and create personas and journey maps
- Design interfaces and wireframes
- Validate usability through testing and feedback
- Participate in design reviews and iterations
- Document design systems and component guidelines

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and support burden
- Build trust and satisfaction with the product

### Typical Communication
- Design review sessions with Product Managers and Developers
- Usability testing reports and iteration feedback
- Design system documentation and component guidelines

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Managers own the deployment schedule and release process, coordinating with all roles to ensure smooth, predictable, and low-risk releases.

### Responsibilities
- Plan and schedule release windows
- Coordinate pre-release checklist completion
- Manage deployment pipeline and orchestration
- Execute deployments and post-deploy verification
- Communicate release status to stakeholders
- Manage rollback procedures if needed

### Goals
- Enable frequent, reliable deployments
- Minimize release-related incidents and downtime
- Maintain clear communication with all stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Release planning and coordination meetings with Developers, QA, Product Managers, and Project Managers
- Deployment status updates and post-release summaries
- Rollback and incident response communication

---

## External Stakeholder (Client Partner)

### Role Summary
External Stakeholders represent customer interests, provide inputs on priorities and requirements, review deliverables, and offer approval. They engage with the delivery team to ensure alignment with business goals.

### Responsibilities
- Define business requirements and success criteria
- Provide feedback on deliverables and prototypes
- Approve major milestones and releases
- Escalate business-level concerns and changes
- Participate in stakeholder reviews and demos
- Report project status to the broader business

### Goals
- Ensure product meets business objectives
- Maintain alignment between delivery and business priorities
- Reduce scope creep and rework through clear requirements

### Typical Communication
- Status updates and milestone reviews with Project Managers
- Priority and requirement discussions with Product Managers
- Demos and feedback sessions with the delivery team

---

## How Personas Integrate with the Project Lifecycle

Each persona plays a distinct role across the stages of a typical OctoAcme project:

| Lifecycle Stage | Key Personas |
|---|---|
| **Project Initiation** | Project Manager, Product Manager, External Stakeholder |
| **Planning** | Project Manager, Product Manager, Developer, QA Lead, UX Designer |
| **Execution & Tracking** | Developer, QA Lead, UX Designer, Project Manager |
| **Release & Deployment** | Release Manager, QA Lead, Developer, Project Manager |
| **Retrospective & Improvement** | All personas contribute feedback and lessons learned |

Cross-functional collaboration is encouraged at every stage. Clear ownership by each persona reduces ambiguity, supports accountability, and accelerates onboarding for new team members.
